Super Happy Dev Day

A Taste of Emerging Software Technology in Charleston

About

When: September 7, 2012

Where: Flagship 2

Details

Super Happy Dev Day is a one day event for the Charleston Software Development Community. Featuring six talks focused on emerging open source software technologies. Each session will be 20 to 30 minutes long and given by a local professional software developer. There will be two sessions, a morning session and an afternoon session. After each session, a 30 minute panel will be held to take questions from the audience.
